    About Us

    Punta Cana is a family-owned Dominican restaurant located in Beverly, Massachusetts.

    We pride ourselves on offering authentic Dominican cuisine in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our menu features a variety of traditional menu__dishes, including tasty mofongo, succulent roast pork, and fresh seafood. We also have a full bar stocked with tropical cocktails.

    Come visit us and experience the flavors of the Dominican Republic right here in Beverly.
